# Closy

**Version:** 0.1
**Status:** Draft

---

## Vision

Closy is an AI-native wardrobe companion that helps people decide what to wear every day.

Instead of manually browsing their wardrobe or searching for outfit inspiration, users interact with a friendly AI stylist that knows their wardrobe and provides personalized recommendations.

Closy aims to reduce decision fatigue, encourage better use of existing clothes, and make getting dressed enjoyable.

---

## Problem

Many people own plenty of clothes but still struggle to decide what to wear.

Common problems include:

- Spending too much time choosing an outfit.
- Forgetting about clothes already owned.
- Buying unnecessary items while missing wardrobe essentials.
- Feeling unsure whether an outfit matches the weather or occasion.

Closy solves these problems by acting as a personal AI stylist rather than a traditional wardrobe manager.

---

## Target Users

Closy is designed for people who:

- have many clothes but struggle to create outfits;
- want to save time every morning;
- enjoy fashion but prefer personalized recommendations;
- like interacting with an AI companion instead of a traditional application.

---

## Product Philosophy

Closy is not a wardrobe database with AI.

**Closy is an AI companion that happens to manage your wardrobe.**

The relationship with the AI should feel warm, personal, and supportive.

Opening Closy should feel more like talking to a trusted stylist than using a utility app.

---

## Core Experience

Users get outfit recommendations through natural conversation with their AI stylist. Early on, this happens on request — the user asks, and Closy answers, using what it knows about their wardrobe.

Over time, this evolves into Closy proactively preparing a recommendation each morning, based on the wardrobe and, eventually, additional context such as weather, calendar events, and personal preferences.

The AI explains why it selected each outfit and can answer follow-up questions naturally — through text, and eventually through voice as well, so the interaction feels like talking to a person rather than querying an app.

---

## AI Companion

The AI stylist is one of the core differentiators of Closy.

The companion:

- has a friendly personality;
- communicates naturally;
- remembers user preferences over time;
- helps build confidence instead of simply returning answers.

Future versions will allow users to choose different companion characters such as:

- Alien
- Cat
- Fox
- Robot
- Fairy

Each companion may have a unique personality, communication style, and visual appearance.

---

## Product Features

### Wardrobe

Users can:

- upload clothing photos;
- organize clothing into categories;
- browse their wardrobe;
- edit or remove clothing items.

### AI Stylist

Users can freely chat with their AI stylist.

Examples:

- What should I wear today?
- Which shoes match this jacket?
- I have dinner tonight.
- Is this outfit too formal?

### Daily Outfit Recommendation

Closy creates personalized outfit recommendations using the user's wardrobe.

Future recommendations may consider:

- weather;
- calendar events;
- preferred styles;
- favorite colors;
- outfit history.

### Wardrobe Insights

Closy helps users better understand their wardrobe.

Examples:

- You're missing a basic white T-shirt.
- You own five black hoodies.
- These sneakers haven't been worn for months.
- You don't have enough winter clothing.

### Outfit Visualization

Future versions will generate AI images showing the recommended outfit.

Users may optionally upload their face so visualizations resemble them.

---

## Future Vision

Closy should evolve into a complete AI fashion companion.

Potential future capabilities include:

- weather-aware recommendations;
- Google Calendar integration;
- shopping recommendations;
- shopping guide;
- packing lists for travel;
- capsule wardrobe planning;
- seasonal wardrobe analysis;
- outfit history and learning from user feedback.

## User Journey

Morning
↓
Closy prepares today's recommendation.
↓
The user opens the app 
↓
The AI stylist presents today's outfit.
↓
The user asks follow-up questions if needed.
↓
The recommendation improves over time as the AI learns the user's preferences.

---

## Design Principles

Closy should feel:

- warm;
- calm;
- personal;
- modern;
- encouraging.

The interface should remain simple and avoid overwhelming users with options.

The AI should always explain its recommendations instead of giving one-word answers.

---

## Success

Closy is successful when users:

- open the app every day;
- trust the AI's recommendations;
- spend less time choosing outfits;
- rediscover clothes they already own;
- feel more confident in what they wear.

The long-term goal is for Closy to become a daily companion.
